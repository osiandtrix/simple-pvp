Features
Everything you need for guild PvP

Built specifically for SimpleMMO guild wars. No fluff, just the tools you need to cycle through targets efficiently.

⚔

Automated War Lists

Fetches active guild wars and their members automatically. Jump into combat with one click.

▶

Target Cycling

Hit Space to skip to the next target. A floating overlay provides Back/Next controls when you need them.

🚫

Guild Blocklist

Block specific guilds from your war list so their members are skipped during combat sessions.

⚙

Level Filtering

Set minimum and maximum level thresholds to only target players within your preferred range.

📈

Combat Stats

Live combat log with kills-per-hour tracking, attack history, and real-time API rate limit monitoring.

⚡

Quick Navigation

One-click buttons for Food, Healer, and Energy pages during combat. No need to leave the app.
